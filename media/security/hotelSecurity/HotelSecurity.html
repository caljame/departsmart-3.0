Hotel Security

  • Hotel guests are targeted for theft or violent crimes.
  • Perpetrators can follow you to your room door and force their way in.
  • Criminals can gain access by posing as part of room service or hotel security.
  • Criminals can force open the door and invade the room violently.
  • Hotels lobbies, restaurants, pools and other recreational areas may have little to no access control.
  • Hotel location may be in a high crime area.
