Medical Evacuation

To request MEDEVAC funding or to notify CDC of MEDEVAC:

  1. E-mail: medevac@cdc.gov*
  2. Phone: 1-770-488-7100 - CDC 24/7 Emergency Operations Center (EOC)
  3. Cable: CDC ATLANTA GA

    To provide prompt response, CDC must be advised via cable or e-mail to medevac@cdc.gov:

    • The name of the patient.
    • The name of the employee.
    • Whether the MEDEVAC is for an emergency, acute, routine, or dental condition.
    • Location of employee.
    • MEDEVAC destination, including name of facility.
    • Dates of MEDEVAC.
    • Name of medical or non-medical attendant, if required.
    • Estimated costs, if known.
    • Name of TRICARE coordinator (if Commissioned Corps Officer of family member).

When the e-mail is received, CGH will respond to DOS to authorize the MEDEVAC and will follow up the next business day with the specifics of funding mechanisms (post held funds available, funds to be cable to post from headquarters, etc.).
