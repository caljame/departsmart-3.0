Povidine-Iodine Antiseptic Solution (10%)

Use for

  • Abrasions
  • Minor burns
  • Minor cuts
  • Scrapes

Application

  • Cleanse skin with soap and water and dry thoroughly.
  • Apply to affected areas 1–3 times daily.
  • DO NOT use longer than 1 week.

Warnings

  • DO NOT use in the eyes.
  • DO NOT use on large, open wounds without a physician’s direction.
  • DO NOT use if you are allergic or sensitive to iodine.
  • STOP> using the solution and GET MEDICAL HELP if fever, redness, irritation, pain, or swelling occurs.
