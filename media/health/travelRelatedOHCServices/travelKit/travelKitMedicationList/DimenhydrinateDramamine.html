Dimenhydrinate (Dramamine©/Driminate)

Take for prevention and treatment of motion sickness or associated symptoms, such as:

  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Dizziness

Adult dosage

  • Take one or two (50mg) tablets every four to six hours, as needed.
  • Take first dose 30–60 minutes before traveling.
  • DO NOT take more than eight (50mg) tablets in a 24 hour period.

Possible side effects

  • Drowsiness
  • Impaired coordination
  • Dry mouth and throat
  • Difficult urination
  • Blurred vision
  • Tremors

Warnings

  • AVOID alcohol, sedatives, and tranquilizers when taking this medication, as these combinations may increase drowsiness.
  • Use caution when driving a motor vehicle or operating machinery.
