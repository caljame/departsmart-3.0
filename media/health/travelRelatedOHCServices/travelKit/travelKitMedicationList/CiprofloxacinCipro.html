Ciprofloxacin (CiproÂ©)

Important note: This medication requires a prescription. Take this medication only on the guidance of a CDC clinic physician or your health care provider.

Potential uses

  • Infectious diarrhea
  • Skin infections
  • Certain other infections

Adult dosage

  • Take one (500 mg) tablet every 12 hours for most infections.
  • Drink plenty of fluids while taking this medication.
  • DO NOT use with blood thinners such as Coumadin, antacids, calcium, or iron.

Possible side effects

  • Gastrointestinal upset
  • Headache
  • Neurologic symptoms (stiff neck, confusion, lethargy)
  • Rash, allergy, sensitivity to sunlight
  • Muscle aches, tendonitis, traumatic tendon rupture
  • Increased effects of caffeine
  • Warning

If you are taking medication for another chronic illness, discuss the use of this medication with your health care provider to determine if significant drug interactions exist.
