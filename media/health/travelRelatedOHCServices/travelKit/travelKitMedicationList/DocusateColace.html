Take for constipation Adult dosage

  • Take one or two (100 mg) soft gels once daily.
  • Drink plenty of clear liquid.
  • Swallow soft gels whole.
  • DO NOT take more than four (100 mg) soft gels in a 24 hour period.
  • DO NOT chew or crust soft gels.

Warnings

  • DO NOT take this medication if you are having more than the mild abdominal pains that may accompany constipation.
  • GET MEDICAL HELP if you experience abdominal pain with fever or prolonged or severe abdominal pain.

Take Note

  • Dehydration is one of the most common causes of constipation. Keep well hydrated during travel.
