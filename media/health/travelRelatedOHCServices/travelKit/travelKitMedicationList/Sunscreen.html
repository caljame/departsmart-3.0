Sunscreen

Use for

  • Prevention of sunburn.

Application

  • Apply approximately 30 minutes before sun exposure.
  • Reapply after strenuous activity or swimming.
  • Use on all parts of your skin exposed to the sun, including the ears, back, shoulders, and the back of the knees and legs.
  • Apply thickly and thoroughly.
  • As a rule of thumb, use an ounce (a handful) to cover your entire body.

Warnings

  • DO NOT use in the eyes.
  • DO NOT use on puncture wounds, animal bites, or serious burns.
  • STOP using this medication and GET MEDICAL HELP if fever, redness, irritation, pain, or swelling occurs.
