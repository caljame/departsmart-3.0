Antibacterial Ointment or Cream

(Neomycin, Polymyxin, Bacitracin combination or other medications alone or in combination)

Use for

  • Abrasions
  • Minor burns
  • Minor cuts
  • Scrapes

Application

  • Cleanse skin with soap and water and dry thoroughly.
  • Apply to affected areas one to three times daily.
  • DO NOT use longer than one week.

Warnings

  • DO NOT use in the eyes.
  • DO NOT use on puncture wounds, animal bites, or serious burns.
  • STOP using this medication and GET MEDICAL HELP if fever, redness, irritation, pain, or swelling occurs.
