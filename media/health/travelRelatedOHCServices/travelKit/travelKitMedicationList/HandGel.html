Hand Gel/Sanitizer or Antiseptic Hand Gel

Use for

  • Cleaning hands when soap and water are not available.

Application

  • Apply product to the palm of one hand.
  • Rub hands together.
  • Rub the product over all surfaces of hands and fingers until dry.

Warnings

  • DO NOT use in the eyes.
  • DO NOT use on puncture wounds, animal bites, or serious burns.
  • STOP using this medication and GET MEDICAL HELPs if fever, redness, irritation, pain, or swelling occurs.
