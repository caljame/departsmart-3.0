Oral Rehydration Salts

Take for rehydration (after diarrhea and/ or vomiting)

Directions

  • Dissolve one 27.9 g package in one liter of drinking water.
  • Discard any unused solution after 24 hours.

No side effects

Warnings

  • DO NOT consume salts in any other manner, except by drinking a solution that is prepared exactly according to package directions.
  • DO NOT over-concentrate or over-dilute this solution; prepare and use exactly as directed.
  • DO NOT boil the solution after mixing.
