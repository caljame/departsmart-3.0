Metronidazole (Flagyl©)

Important Note: This medication requires a prescription. Take this medication only on the guidance of a CDC clinic physician or your health care provider.

Potential uses

  • Giardiasis
  • Other infections as directed by a physician

Adult dosage

  • Take 250 mg three times a day for five days.

Possible side effects

  • Unpleasant metallic taste
  • Nausea
  • Headache
  • Loss of appetite
  • Seizures, neurologic symptoms (stiff neck, confusion, lethargy), in rare instances

Warnings

  • DO NOT use for initial treatment of traveler’s diarrhea.
  • DO NOT drink alcohol while taking metronidazole and for three days after stopping it.
  • AVOID taking metronidazole if you could be pregnant or if you are in the first trimester of pregnancy, unless directed to do so by a medical provider.
