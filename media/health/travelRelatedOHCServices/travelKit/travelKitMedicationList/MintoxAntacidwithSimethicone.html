Mintox© (Antacid with Simethicone)

Take for

  • Heartburn
  • Indigestion
  • Acid stomach
  • Intestinal gas
  • Mild gastritis

Adult dosage

  • Take one to four tablets every four hours, as needed.
  • Chew tablets.

Possible side effects

  • Diarrhea
  • Constipation

Warnings

  • CAUTION: Space antacids six (6) hours before or two (2) hours after doxycycline or ciprofloxacin.

Take Note

  • The dosage suggested above is for Mintox© only, not for other antacids. Follow label or package directions carefully when using other antacids.
