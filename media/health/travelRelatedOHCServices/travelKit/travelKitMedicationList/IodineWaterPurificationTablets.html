Iodine Water Purification Tablets (Potable Aqua Tablets)

Use for

  • Water purification

Application

  • Follow POTABLE AQUA Emergency Drinking Water Germicide Tablets directions on bottle: Add 2 tablets to 1 quart of liter of water capping loosely to allow a small amount of leakage. Wait 5 mins. Shake container allowing screw threads on closure to be moistened, then tighten cap. Wait 30 mins before drinking.
  • The wax seal on the bottle will not damage tablets.

Warnings
  • Use iodine tablets for water purification ONLY when other options are not available!
  • AVOID EYE CONTACT. This substance can cause severe irritation to eyes and mucous membranes!
  • WASH HANDS after handling and before eating, drinking, chewing gum, or using tobacco products.
  • DO NOT use if you could be pregnant.
