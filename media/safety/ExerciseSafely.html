Staying Safe While Exercising in Public

  • Run or walk with others. There is safety in numbers.
  • Let someone know your route and when you expect to be back.
  • Wear bright reflective clothing to help you stand out.
  • Try to pick a route with houses or stores on both sides of the road and in areas with no heavy brush or woods that provide cover for thieves.
  • Do not wear your hair in a ponytail when out exercising, since criminals can more easily latch on and grab or drag you.
  • Do not run or walk in the dark.
